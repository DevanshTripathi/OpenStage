OpenStage provides a platform to Artists to connect with the audience, and also gives people the space to explore various Artists and watch anyone they wish to Watch. The Big Budget Arts have been digitally reaching various People but the small Budget Art performed on Individual level has been unable to make its impression on Society. With the Platform in their hand they can connect to a wide variety of Audience and also improvise upon their work. Thus, we will be able to empower the local Artists with connectivity and give them an openstage to Produce Quality and Authentic Content, and Entertain their Audience.
